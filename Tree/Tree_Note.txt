Why Used Tree?
    1 - For Insert and Delete is o(log N) time.(Tree should be "Self Balance Binary Tree")
    2 - Order Storage (Means all lest node are smaller node and all right node are bigger node).
    3 - Cost Efficeint (Means auto sorted).

Where it is used?
